Shortly About Me:

Milan Buttke strives to capture those special and precise moments whether she's shooting weddings, head shots, portraits, or other events. With an easy going personality and professionalism, she gets the job done right. She also has 19 years experience modeling and acting, so she has a strong understanding of the industry and what it calls for.

With her primary camera being a Nikon D7100, she secondarily shoots with an Olympus E510, and thirdly on her Pentax K1000 35mm film camera. She also has a passion for medium and large format film, and experience with Photoshop CS3 and CS4, Bridge, and Final Cut Pro and Casablanca editing software. Milan has also been a photographer's assistant on numerous occasions in Santa Rosa, Oakland, and San Anselmo, CA.
